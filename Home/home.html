Dear Freshies,
Congratulations on making it into one of the world's most prestigious educational institutions! Your hard work, sleepless nights, and endless dedication have led you to this exciting phase of your life. Just like those who have come before you, there may be uncertainties and challenges along the way, but rest assured, it will be a beautiful journey.
Here at IIT Madras, we believe in providing a holistic educational experience that encompasses academics, sports, and cultural activities. We offer a multitude of opportunities for you to explore your true interests and passions. Through various events and programs, we aim not only to bring out your hidden talents but also to develop your organizational and leadership skills.
Moreover, IIT Madras creates an environment that encourages personal growth and excellence in every aspect of life. Your experiences and outcomes may vary based on the decisions you make, but in the end, the overall picture will always be a beautiful one.
Welcome to our family at IIT Madras! We are thrilled to have you join us on this remarkable journey of self-discovery and achievement.

Welcome to the family!

Core Values

About

In pursuit of its mission IITM will-

  • Develop human resources to serve the nation
  • Recognize teaching as a unifying activity
  • Nurture integrity, creativity, and academic freedom
  • Retain a willingness to experiment with new paradigms
Missions
  • To create and sustain a community of learning in which students acquire knowledge and learn to apply it professionally with due conside ation for ethical, ecological, and economic issues.
  • To pursue research and disseminate research findings.
  • To provide knowledge-based technological services to satisfy the needs of society and the industry.
  • To help in building national capabilities in science, technology, humanities, management, education and research.
Vision

To be an academic institution in dynamic equilibrium with its social, ecological, and economic environment striving continuously for excellence in the fields of education, research and technological service to the nation.
